Let's Drink

Let's Drink is a specialized alcohol search service. We find for you cocktail recipes based on ingredients or by recipes, and suggest alcohols. We also evaluate these recipes and alcohols based on ingredients to create flavor profiles, such sweet to find the perfect drink for you. We plan to aim our service at cocktail lovers, such as college students and bartenders. To accomplish our service, we plan on crawling several cocktail recipe websites, such as drinksmixer.com and liquor.com. Let's Drink is the ultimate drink tool that finds the perfect drink for you.
