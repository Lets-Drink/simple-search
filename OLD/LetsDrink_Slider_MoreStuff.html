Hi Tech is renowned for complex value propositions, jargon & TLA's (Three Letter Acronyms). SketchMotion can help you cut through the clutter & deliver a simple and focused message that people can enjoy. Simple scenarios & storytelling help to simplify the message & make it stick.

MEDICAL

Preventative medicine, medical procedures, medical insurance & medical equipment sales, all face the challenge of simplifying essential communications so that they my be heard and understood. SketchMotion can help illustrate the procedures and benefits of preventative care & insurance, help to explain medical procedures in layman’s terms and help communicate the complex messages required when selling medical equipment.

EDUCATION

The importance of targeting the different learning styles of different students is well documented. SketchMotion targets the learning styles of visual students through both pictures and text, auditory students & to some extent kinesthetic students, while the combination of mediums also delivers maximum impact for any particular individual.

POLITICS

Successful political communications must to the point, simple, emotionally engaging and immediately compelling. The important issues of government are often complex and boring, and can lack obvious connections to everyday life. SketchMotion videos combined with effective scenarios and stories, create impactful communications which resonate & stick in the minds of your audience - the voters.
